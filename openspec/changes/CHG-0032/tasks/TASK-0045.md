## Risk

- **ACGR may continue at high level into Wave 6.** The 75 Wave-6 raw findings include many that look structurally distinct from prior themes (especially PERSRETRO's retrospective lens and STRUCTGOV's amendment-overlay pattern). The §8.1 prediction projected Wave 6 ACGR 10-25%; pending consolidation the actual number could be higher. Per user direction this is acceptable — the convergence-threshold question is to be resolved by signal-exhaustion observation across multiple waves, not by hitting a pre-defined ACGR threshold.
- **SCENNOW and PERSRETRO methods may not cluster cleanly.** Their outputs have a different shape than prior streams: SCENNOW reports per-actor-scenario; PERSRETRO reports per-regret. The existing theme taxonomy is finding-shaped, not narrative-shaped. The methodology ADR should consider whether actor-scenario findings warrant a sub-axis (e.g., Axis F = "actor experience surface") or remain folded into Axis A (Lens).
- **PERSRETRO contains some findings that the architecture, as a 2026 artifact, may not be reasonably expected to address.** Specifically PERSRETRO-SER-006's "hooks ran outside transactions" hindsight requires knowledge of three years of hook failures that 2026 doesn't have. The next consolidation pass should flag such items as `PROC` (process-regret) rather than `CRIT` (actively-cost-the-project) when re-attributing severity.
- **Two Wave-6 streams surfaced concrete extant drift in the architecture-as-written**: STRUCTGOV-PROC-005 (`skill-removal-acknowledged` cross-document mismatch) and STRUCTGOV-CRIT-001 (three documents naming the BMAD substrate with no canonical resolver). These are not pre-mortems — they are facts about the current artifacts. The consolidation pass should treat them as immediate-action CRIT/SER not deferred-retrospective.
- **The signal-exhaustion criterion remains undefined.** Per TASK-0044 risk note, no operational definition exists. Wave 6 produced 75 raw findings — at least as voluminous as Wave 5's 84 (excluding META). The audit has NOT run out of signal; the user direction's terminal condition has not yet triggered.
